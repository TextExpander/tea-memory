When someone refers to the "Staging database" or "staging db", they mean the `qa` database on the `Tenet-CI` cluster (MongoDB Atlas).

**Why:** Established by Hernan Calabrese on 2026-07-03 to disambiguate terminology.
**How to apply:** Route staging-db questions/queries to the `qa` database on the `Tenet-CI` Atlas cluster.
